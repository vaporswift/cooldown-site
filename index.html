What it does

FREE
  • Live session (5-hour) and weekly limit percentages
  • Exact reset countdowns
  • "Sonnet only" weekly breakdown, matching Claude's own usage panel
  • Your plan at a glance (Pro, Max, Team)
  • Home screen, Lock Screen, and macOS desktop widgets
  • Menu bar app for Mac — zero setup if you use Claude Code
COOLDOWN PRO
  • "Back to work" alerts the instant a maxed-out limit resets
  • Dynamic Island countdown — a live timer to your reset
  • Full cost analytics — daily and all-time spend, by model

Private by design

Cooldown collects nothing. There's no account, no analytics, and no server — your usage data and credentials stay on your device and in your own iCloud. See the Privacy Policy for details.
